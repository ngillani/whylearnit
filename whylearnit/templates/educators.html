Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is to provide packets addressed in two dimensions: interest level, and topic addressed (mapped to core standards). We don't hit every interest with every topic, nor every topic with a single interest. We hope though, to at least have a choice of several interests for any one topic, and vice versa.
