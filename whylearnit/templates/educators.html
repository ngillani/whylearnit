Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is to provide packets addressed in two dimensions: interest level, and topic addressed (mapped to core standards). We don't hit every interest with every topic, nor every topic with a single interest. We hope though, to at least have a choice of several interests for any one topic, and vice versa. Common Core Standards

We've related all of our packets back to the common core standards for high school math.

Sports: the sports packet has an emphasis on modeling, correlation, and inference from datasets. (Common core standards N-Q.1, F-IF.4, S-ID.3, S-ID.6, S-ID.9).

Videogames: the videogames packet tries to convey the flexibility and power of modeling physical systems with position, velocity, and acceleration. (Common core standards N-Q.1, A-SSE.1, A-CED.1, F-BF.1, F-LE.2, F-LE.5).

Finance: the finance packet focuses on graphical analysis, prediction, and informed decision making. (Common core standards F-IF.6, F-LE.5, S-ID.7).

Music: music touches on graphical analysis, function composition, and the interpretation of functions as sound. (Common core standards F-IF.7).
